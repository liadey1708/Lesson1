Welcome To Apex Friendship High School

Apex, NC

Apex Friendship is a comprehensive high school in Wake County, which opened in August for the 2015-2016 school year.

Here are some quick facts about our school: It has 111 classrooms. It is 315,752 sq.ft. and sits on 108 acres.
Our 2022-2023 enrollment is estimated at about 2,800 students.
Our 2022-2023 staff includes about 140 teachers, 14 instructional assistants, 2 media specialists, 9 counselors, 8 administrators, and 11 support staff members.

Our school is committed to working with each of our students to create meaningful opportunities following graduation and the skills to make the most of those opportunities.
We invite our students, parents, and community to help build an environment where all students are happy, healthy, and safe so that they can experience four years of growth and achievement!
